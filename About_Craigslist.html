What is the point?

Craigslist was origionally created to spread information about cool events around San Francisco.
The site expanded as people began to post different items, jobs, stuff for sale, and apartments.
Craigslist has transformed in many ways over the years. It has grown to exist in 70 countries, with a center in every state in the US. Craigslist has expanded in some regards and shrunk in others to meet consumer demands.
Craigslist remains today a non-commevial site intended to expand people's virtual community.

  • Listing items for sale
  • Listing or finding apartments
  • Getting the word out about events
  • Making important connections
